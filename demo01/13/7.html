Part A. Information-Centric

    • [Milestone Actions] Issue government-wide open data, content, and web API policy and identify standards and best practices for improved interoperability.

    • [Milestone Actions] Ensure all new IT systems follow the open data, content, and web API policy and operationalize agency.gov/developer pages. [Within 6 months of release of open data policy � see milestone 1.1]. [ref. Req. 15.05]

    • [Milestone Actions] Make high-value data and content in at least two existing major customer-facing systems available through web APIs, apply metadata tagging and publish a plan to transition additional high-value systems. [Within 6 months of release of open data policy � see milestone 1.1]. [ref. Req. 15.05]
